How It works

A day behind the wheel

When you’re ready to drive, just open the app and trip requests will start coming in. We’ll show you who you’re picking up and provide directions to their location and destination. Once the trip is complete, we’ll find another nearby request. Riders are friendly and respectful of your car. And only you decide when to stop driving for the day.

Earnings

The fares you earn automatically deposit in your bank account every week. Use the app to track how much you make on every trip. For making the app available to you, we deduct a service fee.

Rewards

Safety

We’re dedicated to keeping you safe before, during, and after every trip. You’ll always know who you’re picking up, and fares are automatically charged through the app—so you avoid the risk of carrying cash. Our specially trained incident response teams are available 24/7.
